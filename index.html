FAQ

Common questions.

How long can the tiramisu last outside?

Roughly 4 hours. The tiramisus are packed with ice packs and insulated packaging to keep them fresh during transport.

How long should I keep it in the fridge?

Best consumed within 3 days from collection. Keep the container sealed and refrigerated until ready to serve.

Is the Big Boy suitable for parties?

Absolutely — it serves 12–16 pax and is 4× bigger than the regular size. Perfect for gatherings and office sharing. Available in Original and Biscoff.
